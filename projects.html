Ongoing projects

Derivation and Evaluation of Design Factors for Efficiency of Smart AI CCTV System

Various designs and evaluations of the combination of technical elements and delivery methods of the smart AI CCTV system are intended to derive highly effective system requirements.

Psychotherapy research using AI

This project explores the synergy between artificial intelligence and psychotherapy to improve mental health treatment. Our team is developing AI tools to assist in understanding and interpreting complex human emotions, aiming to enhance therapeutic outcomes.

Conversations between Healthcare virtual agents

Research on the impact on users of virtual agents that communicate their condition to healthcare providers.

Emotional Support Diary

This project focuses on crafting a personal agent that provides emotional support through Large Language Models (LLMs) by interacting with users' daily diaries or journals, thereby improving emotional well-being through thoughtful engagement with recorded experiences.

Past projects

Measuring cognitive load while driving

Developed original technology to analyze driver cognitive load in driving situations to help drivers make better use of their free time.

Learn English while driving based on cognitive load

Developing an application that utilizes cognitive load to help drivers learn English in their free time.

Designing healthcare applications with LLM agents

Designed an application that utilizes LLM to recommend do's and don'ts for patients who are unable to visit a doctor in person.
